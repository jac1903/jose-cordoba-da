# Executive Summary: Sales Performance & Forecasting

**Project**: Sales Performance Dashboard  
**Author**: Jose Cordoba  
**Date**: July 2026  
**Client**: Retail Company (Portfolio Project)

---

## 1. Executive Overview

This report summarizes the sales performance of a retail company operating across 4 regions (North, South, East, West) from 2015 to 2018. Using a combination of **data analysis**, **visualization**, and **forecasting**, I have identified key trends, performance drivers, and future projections to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 2. Key Findings

### 2.1 Sales Trend
- **Peak Period**: Sales peaked in 2016 (~$684 average monthly sales).
- **Decline**: Sales declined significantly from 2017 onward (~$330 average monthly sales, a **-52% drop**).
- **Seasonality**: November shows a slight holiday peak, but the overall trend remains downward.

### 2.2 Regional Performance
- **West Region** generates the highest sales ($45,000+ total).
- **South Region** has the lowest sales (~$18,000 total).
- The West region outperforms the South by approximately **150%**.

### 2.3 Product Categories
- **Technology** is the highest-grossing category.
- **Furniture** and **Office Supplies** follow closely.

### 2.4 Customer Segments
- **Consumer segment** accounts for the largest share of revenue (~68%).
- Corporate and Home Office segments represent the remaining share.

### 2.5 Forecast (3-Month Outlook)
- **Total Forecasted Sales (Nov 2018 – Jan 2019)**: $305.48
- **Average Monthly Sales**: $101.83
- **Peak Month**: November 2018 ($127.70)
- **Lowest Month**: January 2019 ($75.67)
- **Trend**: Slight downward trend into 2019.

---

## 3. Business Insights

### 3.1 What's Working
- **West Region** is outperforming—consider allocating more marketing budget there.
- **Technology** is the strongest category—consider expanding this product line.

### 3.2 Areas of Concern
- **Declining Sales**: The significant drop from 2017 onward requires investigation.
- **South Region**: Underperforming; needs strategic review.

---

## 4. Recommendations

### 4.1 Short-Term (Next 3 Months)
1. **Investigate the decline**: Analyze why sales dropped in 2017 (competitor entry? market changes? product issues?).
2. **Focus on the West region**: Run targeted campaigns to capitalize on momentum.
3. **Monitor forecasted sales**: Compare actual vs forecasted monthly to validate the model.

### 4.2 Long-Term (6-12 Months)
1. **Collect more data**: At least 5+ years of sales data for better seasonality detection.
2. **Add external factors**: Marketing spend, economic indicators, competitor data.
3. **Re-forecast quarterly**: Update projections with new data for better accuracy.

---

## 5. Limitations

The analysis and forecast are based on **limited historical data (2015-2018)**. Key limitations include:

- No profit data available (analysis focuses on sales only).
- Yearly seasonality could not be reliably detected with only 3 years of data.
- External factors (marketing campaigns, economic conditions) are not included.
- Forecast horizon is limited to 3 months for reliability.

---

## 6. Conclusion

This project provides a **comprehensive view** of sales performance, identifies key areas of concern, and offers actionable recommendations. The dashboard serves as a **living tool** that can be updated monthly to support ongoing business decisions.

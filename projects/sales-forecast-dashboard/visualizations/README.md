## 📊 Chart Descriptions

### Sales by Region (`sales_by_region.png`)

**What it shows**: Total sales revenue by geographic region (North, South, East, West).

**Key Insight**: The **West region** generates the highest sales, while the **South region** has the lowest.

**Use Case**: Helps regional managers understand performance and identify areas for improvement.

---

### Sales by Category (`sales_by_category.png`)

**What it shows**: Total sales by product category (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology).

**Key Insight**: **Technology** is the highest-grossing category, followed by Furniture and Office Supplies.

**Use Case**: Helps product managers decide where to focus marketing efforts.

---

### Monthly Sales Trend (`monthly_sales_trend.png`)

**What it shows**: Sales over time (2015-2018), with monthly aggregation.

**Key Insight**: Sales peaked in 2016 and have been declining since 2017. **November** shows a seasonal holiday peak.

**Use Case**: Identifies seasonality and long-term trends for strategic planning.

---

### Sales by Customer Segment (`segment_pie_chart.png`)

**What it shows**: Distribution of sales across customer segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office).

**Key Insight**: **Consumers** account for the largest share of revenue.

**Use Case**: Helps marketing teams target the right segments with the right messaging.

---

### Sales Boxplot by Category (`sales_boxplot.png`)

**What it shows**: Distribution of individual order values by category (min, max, median, outliers).

**Key Insight**: **Technology** has the widest range of order values, with some very high-value orders.

**Use Case**: Helps understand order value variation and identify high-value opportunities.

---

### Top 10 Products (`top_products.png`)

**What it shows**: The 10 best-selling products by total sales revenue.

**Key Insight**: Furniture and Technology products dominate the top 10 list.

**Use Case**: Guides inventory management and marketing promotions.

---

### Sales Forecast (`forecast_plot.png`)

**What it shows**: Prophet forecast for 3 months (November 2018 – January 2019) with confidence intervals.

**Key Insight**: Sales are projected to continue the slight decline into 2019.

**⚠️ Important**: This forecast is based on limited historical data (2015-2018).

**Use Case**: Supports short-term inventory and staffing decisions.

---

### Forecast Components (`forecast_components.png`)

**What it shows**: Breakdown of the forecast into:
- **Trend**: Overall direction of sales
- **Yearly Seasonality**: Repeating yearly patterns
- **Holiday Effects**: Short-term spikes

**Key Insight**: The trend is declining, and there's a slight seasonal bump in November.

**Use Case**: Helps understand what's driving the forecast (trend vs. seasonality).

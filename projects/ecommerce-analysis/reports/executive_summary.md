\# E-commerce Customer Analysis — Executive Summary



\*\*Date\*\*: July 2026  

\*\*Author\*\*: Jose Cordoba  



\## Business Challenge

The e-commerce company is experiencing a 25% annual churn rate. Marketing needs to identify which customers to target and with what messages.



\## Methodology

\- Analyzed 100,000+ orders using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis

\- Segmented customers into 5 distinct groups: Champions, Loyal, Potential, At-Risk, Churned



\## Key Findings



\### 1. Customer Concentration

\- \*\*Top 5% of customers (Champions)\*\* generate 35% of total revenue

\- \*\*80% of revenue\*\* comes from just 40% of customers



\### 2. Retention Opportunities

\- Average repeat purchase rate is \*\*18%\*\* — potential to increase to 30%

\- \*\*Health \& Beauty\*\* category has 40% higher retention



\### 3. Regional Growth

\- \*\*Northeast region\*\* growing 15% faster than national average



\## Recommendations



\### Immediate Actions

1\. \*\*VIP Program\*\* — Launch exclusive offers for Champions

2\. \*\*Win-back Campaign\*\* — Target At-Risk customers with 15% discount

3\. \*\*Cross-sell Strategy\*\* — Promote Health \& Beauty to Loyal customers



\### Long-term Strategy

1\. Implement automated RFM scoring monthly

2\. Build predictive churn model (Machine Learning)

3\. Expand marketing in Northeast region
